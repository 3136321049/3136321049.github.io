ClinConsensus是一个由临床专家验证的中文医疗LLM基准，涵盖全病程护理的2500个开放案例，支持可靠评估。

核心贡献

提出了ClinConsensus基准和Clinically Applicable Consistency Score (CACS@k)评估指标，建立了双评委评估框架结合LLM-as-judge和本地部署模型。

方法概述

采用基于评分标准的评估协议，构建了涵盖36个医学专科、12种临床任务类型的2500个开放案例。通过专家验证和双评委框架确保评估的可靠性和可扩展性。

关键结论

顶级模型在总体得分上相当，但在推理、证据使用和长期随访能力上差异显著，临床可操作的治疗规划仍是关键瓶颈。

原文摘录

Large language models (LLMs) are increasingly applied to health management, showing promise across disease prevention, clinical decision-making, and long-term care. However, existing medical benchmarks remain largely static and task-isolated, failing to capture the openness, longitudinal structure, and safety-critical complexity of real-world clinical workflows. We introduce ClinConsensus, a Chinese medical benchmark curated, validated, and quality-controlled by clinical experts. ClinConsensus comprises 2500 open-ended cases spanning the full continuum of care--from prevention and intervention to long-term follow-up--covering 36 medical specialties, 12 common clinical task types, and progressively increasing levels of complexity. To enable reliable evaluation of such complex scenarios, we adopt a rubric-based grading protocol and propose the Clinically Applicable Consistency Score (CACS@k).
