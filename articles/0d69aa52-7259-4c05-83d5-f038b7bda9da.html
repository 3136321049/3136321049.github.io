提出递归思考-回答过程(R-TAP)，通过迭代推理和置信度评估提升LLM和VLM的推理准确性

核心贡献

开发了R-TAP方法，使模型能够进行迭代推理循环并生成更准确的答案。通过置信度生成器和双重奖励机制，显著提升了模型推理性能。

方法概述

R-TAP包含置信度生成器来评估模型响应的确定性并指导后续改进。采用递归置信度增加奖励和最终答案置信度奖励两种互补奖励机制。通过分析Oops类表达式的频率来评估推理稳定性。

关键结论

R-TAP增强的模型在LLM和VLM上均优于传统单次推理方法，表现出更少的自反性模式和更稳定的推理过程。该方法为未来AI推理过程的精细化提供了有效途径。

原文摘录

Think-Answer reasoners such as DeepSeek-R1 have made notable progress by leveraging interpretable internal reasoning. However, despite the frequent presence of self-reflective cues like "Oops!", they remain vulnerable to output errors during single-pass inference. To address this limitation, we propose an efficient Recursive Think-Answer Process (R-TAP) that enables models to engage in iterative reasoning cycles and generate more accurate answers, going beyond conventional single-pass approaches. Central to this approach is a confidence generator that evaluates the certainty of model responses and guides subsequent improvements. By incorporating two complementary rewards-Recursively Confidence Increase Reward and Final Answer Confidence Reward-we show that R-TAP-enhanced models consistently outperform conventional single-pass methods for both large language models (LLMs) and vision-language models (VLMs).
