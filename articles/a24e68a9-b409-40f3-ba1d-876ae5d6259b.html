OmniRet是首个支持文本、视觉和音频三模态检索的高效模型，通过注意力重采样和Wasserstein池化提升表示质量

核心贡献

提出了首个支持三模态（文本、视觉、音频）的检索模型，解决了多模态检索中的计算效率和表示保真度两大关键挑战

方法概述

采用注意力重采样机制从模态特定编码器中生成紧凑的固定大小表示，提出注意力切片Wasserstein池化来保留细粒度细节，避免信息丢失

在13个检索任务上表现优异，特别是在组合查询、音频和视频检索任务上显著提升，同时在其他任务上与SOTA模型性能相当

原文摘录

Multimodal retrieval is the task of aggregating information from queries across heterogeneous modalities to retrieve desired targets. State-of-the-art multimodal retrieval models can understand complex queries, yet they are typically limited to two modalities: text and vision. This limitation impedes the development of universal retrieval systems capable of comprehending queries that combine more than two modalities. To advance toward this goal, we present OmniRet, the first retrieval model capable of handling complex, composed queries spanning three key modalities: text, vision, and audio. Our OmniRet model addresses two critical challenges for universal retrieval: computational efficiency and representation fidelity. First, feeding massive token sequences from modality-specific encoders to Large Language Models (LLMs) is computationally inefficient.
